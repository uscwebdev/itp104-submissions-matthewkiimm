Project Summary: London & Paris Activities

This website encapsulates my travel experiences in London, Paris, and Amsterdam, focusing on the diverse activities and culinary explorations encountered. It serves to share these cultural and historical experiences with a wider audience, providing a window into the unique aspects of each city. The project employs responsive web design for optimal viewing across various devices and uses advanced CSS techniques enhance the site's aesthetic appeal and usability, ensuring a seamless user experience. The aim is to offer a virtual tour of these cities, highlighting their landmarks and gastronomic delights through a personal narrative lens.
